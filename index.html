Ethanol (C2H5OH)

Essential Safety & Usage Information

Chemical Name: Ethanol
Formula: C2H5OH
pH Level: ~7.3 (neutral to slightly basic)
7.3
Hazard Status:
Generally Safe (Flammable)
Can Touch with Bare Hands?
✅ Yes – But avoid prolonged skin contact
Carcinogenic (Cancer Causing)?
⚠ Yes – Prolonged high consumption may increase cancer risk
3D Molecular Structure:
Common Uses:
  • Used as an industrial solvent and cleaning agent
  • Main component of alcoholic beverages
  • Used in hand sanitizers and disinfectants
  • Fuel additive in vehicles
Safety Instructions:
  • Highly flammable – keep away from open flames
  • Use in well-ventilated areas
  • Avoid prolonged skin contact
  • Do not ingest in high quantities
  • Store in a tightly closed container
Interesting Fact: Ethanol is renewable and can be produced through the fermentation of sugars, making it a popular biofuel.
